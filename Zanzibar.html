Stone Town – Zanzibar

Historical, cultural, and architectural treasure

About Stone Town

Stone Town is the old part of Zanzibar City, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It’s known for its mix of Arab, Persian, Indian, and European influences. Walk through narrow alleys, visit spice markets, and admire carved wooden doors and mosques.

How to Reach

You can fly directly to Zanzibar from major cities like Dar es Salaam or Arusha. There are also ferries from Dar es Salaam. Once on the island, Stone Town is easily reachable by taxi or local transport.
