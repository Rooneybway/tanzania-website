Serengeti National Park

World-famous for the Great Migration and big cats

About Serengeti

The Serengeti is one of the most iconic wildlife parks in the world. It hosts the Great Migration – where millions of wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles move in search of water. You’ll also see lions, cheetahs, elephants, and much more.

How to Reach

Serengeti is about 7–8 hours by road from Arusha or can be accessed via local flights to Seronera Airstrip. Most safaris include Serengeti in their routes.
