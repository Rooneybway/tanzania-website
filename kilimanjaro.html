Kilimanjaro National Park

Home to Africa’s highest peak – Mount Kilimanjaro

About Kilimanjaro

Mount Kilimanjaro is Africa’s tallest mountain and a popular climbing destination. The park offers stunning scenery – from rainforests to alpine deserts and snowy peaks. You don’t need climbing experience for some routes, but good fitness helps.

How to Reach

Fly to Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO), then drive to Moshi or Marangu gate. Tour operators offer guided climbs and day hikes.
