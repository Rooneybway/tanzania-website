Olduvai Gorge

Cradle of Mankind – A world-famous archaeological site

About Olduvai Gorge

Located in the Great Rift Valley, Olduvai Gorge is one of the most important prehistoric sites in the world. It has yielded numerous fossils and tools from early humans, helping scientists understand our origins.

How to Reach

Olduvai is located between Ngorongoro and Serengeti. You can reach it by 4x4 vehicle through the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Tours often include it in multi-day safari packages.
